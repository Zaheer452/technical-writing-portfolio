# Integrating TaskForge with Your Workflow

Welcome to TaskForge! This guide will help you get started quickly by connecting TaskForge with your existing workflow. Follow these steps to onboard efficiently and avoid common pitfalls.

---

## Overview
TaskForge helps teams manage tasks, projects, and deadlines efficiently. This guide covers:
- Account setup
- Creating your first project
- Integrating TaskForge with your tools
- Troubleshooting common issues

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Go to [https://taskforge.com/signup](https://taskforge.com/signup)
2. Enter your **name**, **email**, and **password**
3. Click **Sign Up**
4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Project
1. Click **New Project** in the dashboard
2. Enter a project **name** and optional **description**
3. Assign team members (optional)
4. Click **Create Project**

> Tip: Use clear project names to make task management easier.

---

## Step 3: Add Tasks
1. Within your project, click **Add Task**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title**
   - **Description**
   - **Due date**
   - **Assign to team member** (optional)
3. Click **Save Task**

---

## Step 4: Integrate with Your Tools
TaskForge can integrate with popular apps:
- **Slack:** Receive task updates in your channels  
- **Google Calendar:** Sync deadlines automatically  
- **Zapier:** Automate workflows with custom triggers  

**Example: Integrate with Slack**
1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Slack**
2. Click **Connect Workspace**
3. Authorize TaskForge
4. Choose channels to receive updates

---

## Step 5: Troubleshooting
- **Cannot log in:** Reset your password via [https://taskforge.com/reset-password](https://taskforge.com/reset-password)  
- **Tasks not syncing:** Check integration permissions and refresh connections  
- **Missing notifications:** Verify your email preferences in Settings  

---

## FAQ

**Q:** Can I assign a task to multiple team members?  
**A:** Yes, TaskForge allows multiple assignees per task.  

**Q:** How do I delete a project?  
**A:** Go to project settings → Delete Project. This is permanent.  

**Q:** Can I export tasks?  
**A:** Yes, use the **Export CSV** option under the project menu.
